It's Payback Time

by: Ted Carlson

As the Summer sun gives way to crisp Fall air, the end of my Analytics program starts coming into focus, and I start feeling the graduate program version of the "Sunday scaries". These are not caused by Winter, or finding a full-time job, or even "adulting". This is a fear that haunts [insert number]% of all university graduates nationwide--student loans.

Student loans seems like a concept that is as old as time itself. It's not difficult to picture Aristotle logging onto Fedloan to complete his FAFSA, or Confucius checking NerdWallet to a find a list of the best private lenders, but student lending is a rather novel concept.
